This guide walks you through the full flow — from the landing page to your personal dashboard.

<Note>
  If you already have an account, Glint 247 stores your JWT token in `localStorage`. Any page that checks for authentication (the landing page, `/join`, and `/login`) will redirect you straight to `/dashboard` if a valid token is found.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the landing page">
    Open Glint 247 in your browser. You'll see the **HeaderSection** logo and tagline — *Hub For Creative Minds* — followed by a swipeable carousel.

    The carousel has three frames. The first frame shows two calls to action:

    - **Already a Member?** → Click **SIGN IN** to go to `/login`.
    - **New to Glint 247?** → Click **JOIN NOW** to go to `/join`.

    Swipe left or right (or use the arrow buttons on frames 2 and 3) to browse the remaining slides. Dot indicators at the bottom of the card show which frame is active.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an account">
    Navigate to `/join`. You'll see the **Join Us** registration form.

    Fill in the following fields:

    - **Email or phone number** — Enter the email address or phone number you want to use as your username.
    - **Password (8+ characters)** — Choose a password with at least 8 characters. Click the eye icon to toggle visibility.
    - **Remember me** (optional) — Check this box to keep your session preferences.

    Click **Agree & Join**. By submitting the form you agree to Glint 247's user agreement and privacy policy.

    On success, the app registers your account via `POST /api/auth/register`, stores your JWT token, and redirects you to the first onboarding step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding step 1 — your name">
    You'll land on `/onboarding/step1/{your-email}`. The page prompts you to **empower your professional growth** by providing:

    - **First Name** (`firstName`) — Required.
    - **Last Name** (`lastName`) — Required.

    Click **Continue**. Your name is saved to `localStorage` and you move to step 2.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding step 2 — your location">
    You'll land on `/onboarding/step2/{your-email}`. The page greets you by your first name and asks you to connect with your community:

    - **Country/Region** (`region_country`) — Required.
    - **City/District** (`city_district`) — Required.

    Click **Continue**. Your location is saved to `localStorage` and you're redirected to `/dashboard`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    The dashboard greets you with **Welcome to Your Dashboard** and displays four navigation cards:

    | Card | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Profile** | View and edit your profile information |
    | **Settings** | Manage your account settings |
    | **Messages** | Check your latest messages |
    | **Analytics** | View your performance analytics |

    Click any card to navigate to that section. When you're done, click the red **Logout** button at the top of the dashboard to remove your token and return to `/login`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Glint 247 currently stores user profile data — including your name, location, and credentials — in `localStorage`, not a remote database. Clearing your browser data or switching browsers will log you out and remove your profile information. This is a known limitation of the current implementation.
</Warning>
